Setup Guide

Get up and running in under 30 minutes

Sign Up Free

Create your SewReady account. Your custom storefront goes live immediately with your shop name, address, hours, and services.

Set Up Your Dashboard

Upgrade to Online or Full to unlock the admin dashboard. Add employees, configure services, set your schedule, and import inventory.

Connect Hardware

Plug in your receipt printer and label printer via USB. SewReady uses browser-based printing — just click "Print Receipt" from any order.

Share Your Link

Your shop gets a permanent URL at sewing.ranger-beers.com/shops/your-shop/. Share it on Google Business, social media, and at your counter.

Start Taking Orders

Customers place orders online or walk in. You manage everything from the dashboard. Receipts print automatically. Notifications go out when orders are ready.
